ABOUT ME

This project presents a dynamic particle animation that simulates the physical processes of free fall and rebound. Colorful particles generated at the top of the screen fall in a manner reminiscent of free fall, and when they touch the bottom of the screen, they bounce back until their rebound speed reduces to a certain degree, at which point they begin to fall again, forming a cycle of animation. With its vibrant visual effects and realistic physics, this project offers an engaging web experience for users.
